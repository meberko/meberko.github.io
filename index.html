What has he done?

  • EigenProbe

  • ScheduleBot

  • Spidey

  • EigenProbe is a simulation framework built in Python to calculate and depict the propagation of coupled light-matter (polaritonic) modes in 2D quantum materials. This project leverages a spectral approach to the calculation that involve finding the eigenmodes of a modified Helmholtz equation and using them to construct a self-consistent solution.

  • ScheduleBot is a scheduling framework built in Python to automate the process of assigning regularly occuring duties. It was designed during my year as a head teaching assistant of the Columbia Physics Department because no one should have to spend over 10 hours in a semester scheduling students for lab slots.

  • Spidey is my own personal WEBsite (get it?!) built from the ground up in HTML, CSS (using UIKit), Javascript, and Flask. I think he's looking pretty amazing right now but he's a work in progress. Perhaps one day he could even look spectacular or sensational.
